IV.Tone Breaking


Here's a little rundown of the technique known as Breaking.

The most recent technique to be discovered is Tonebreaking, by an artist called 'B4d Wolf'. Tonebreaking is very similar to Breaking, but it's stretched out just before you need to break the tone. The result is a more weirder tone that's broken. It was discovered on June 2nd, 2021.
